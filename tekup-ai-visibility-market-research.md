### The AI Visibility Crisis (Demand-Side Data)

- **ChatGPT recommends only 1.2% of local businesses** — 98.8% are invisible to AI search
- **83% of restaurants** do not appear in ChatGPT recommendations at all
- **Only 45% overlap** between businesses that rank well on Google and those cited by AI
- **AI visibility is 3x to 30x harder** than traditional local search ranking
- **48% of Google queries** now trigger AI Overviews (up from 13.14% in March 2025)
- **Organic CTR has dropped 61%** on AI Overviews-triggered queries
- **45% of consumers** now use AI tools to find local services (up from 6% one year ago)
- **54% of US marketers** plan to implement GEO within 3-6 months
- **74% of small businesses** invest in SEO; average monthly SMB spend is $497/mo

### AI Engine Query Volumes (2026)

| Platform | Monthly Queries |
|---|---|
| ChatGPT (search-style) | 1B+ |
| Perplexity | 800M+ |
| Gemini / Google AI Overviews | Present on 50%+ of searches |

### How Each AI Engine Indexes Differently

- **ChatGPT:** Pulls from Bing, Yelp, TripAdvisor, Wikipedia. Favors encyclopedic content. Only 1.2% of local businesses cited.
- **Perplexity:** Real-time web crawling. Heavily cites Reddit (46.7%). Rewards factual, well-referenced content.
- **Gemini / Google AI Overviews:** Prefers YouTube + multi-modal content. Cross-references GBP data. Weights review sentiment heavily.
- **Bing Copilot:** Microsoft's index. Sites ranking well in Bing organic get more citations.
- **Claude:** Search is opt-in. Cites fewer sources but higher quality matching.

**Critical insight:** Only 11% of domains are cited by BOTH ChatGPT and Perplexity. Multi-platform optimization is essential.

## SECTION 2: COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

### Part A: Traditional Local SEO Competitors

#### BrightLocal
- **Pricing:** From $29/mo (Starter) to $99/mo (Agency)
- **Core Focus:** Citation tracking, review monitoring, local rank tracking, Google Business Profile audit
- **AI Features:** Limited — no GEO/AI visibility tracking
- **Gap:** No AI visibility monitoring, no GEO optimization, no schema generation

#### Yext
- **Pricing:** Custom enterprise pricing (typically $300-$500+/mo per location, annual contracts)
- **Core Focus:** Enterprise listing management across 100+ directories
- **AI Features:** Yext Content (AI-powered content generation). Some schema functionality
- **Gap:** Enterprise pricing locks out SMBs. No dedicated AI visibility auditing

#### Moz Local
- **Pricing:** From $16/mo (Single) to $39/mo (Multi-location)
- **Core Focus:** Automated listing sync, review monitoring, duplicate detection
- **AI Features:** Minimal
- **Gap:** No GEO/AEO features. No AI visibility tracking

#### Semrush (Semrush Local)
- **Pricing:** From $140/mo (Pro) to $500+/mo (Business). Local-specific: $30-60/mo per location
- **Core Focus:** Full SEO suite + listing distribution via Yext network
- **AI Features:** "AI Mentions" tracking for ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews (added 2025)
- **Gap:** AI tracking is shallow. No direct citation optimization for AI engines

### Part B: AI-Specific Competitors (Dedicated AI Visibility Tools)

#### Profound (tryprofound.com)
- **Pricing:** ~$500/mo (small business) to 4-figures (agency)
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot
- **Features:** Citation share tracking, sentiment analysis, competitor benchmarking, daily automated checks
- **Limitations:** Expensive for single-practice use. Coverage gaps

#### Otterly.AI
- **Pricing:** ~$129/mo (small business tier)
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overviews
- **Features:** Dedicated AI search visibility tracking. Surfaces "missed opportunities"
- **Limitations:** Smaller engine list. Less polished UI

#### AthenaHQ
- **Pricing:** ~$200/mo
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Bing Copilot, Claude
- **Features:** Source-level analysis (which specific pages are cited)
- **Limitations:** Newer entrant. Engine coverage still growing

#### Peec AI (peec.ai)
- **Pricing:** €89-€299/mo depending on tracked query volume
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Perplexity (primary focus)
- **Features:** Prompt suggestion tools, brand mention tracking
- **Limitations:** Focused on major 3 engines only

#### Brand24
- **Pricing:** $79-$249/mo
- **Coverage:** General brand monitoring + AI search add-on
- **Features:** Tracks traditional sources AND AI engines
- **Limitations:** AI search is an add-on. Depth is shallower

#### HubSpot AI Search Grader
- **Pricing:** FREE
- **Coverage:** Major AI engines (one-time check)
- **Features:** Quick comparison report
- **Limitations:** One-time check only

#### Ahrefs Brand Radar
- **Pricing:** Bundled with Ahrefs ($129/mo+)
- **Coverage:** AI Overviews (strongest), ChatGPT
- **Features:** Pairs with Ahrefs' backlink/organic data
- **Limitations:** Less depth than dedicated tools

#### Semrush AI Mentions
- **Pricing:** Bundled with Semrush ($140/mo+)
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews
- **Features:** Integrated into Semrush SEO dashboard
- **Limitations:** Less depth than Profound or AthenaHQ

#### Local Falcon (AI Visibility Feature)
- **Pricing:** From $29/mo
- **Coverage:** Added AI visibility tracking feature in 2025/2026
- **Features:** Geo-grid rank tracking, now AI visibility monitoring
- **Limitations:** New feature, depth unclear

### Part C: Open-Source / DIY Alternatives

#### Aperture (anyin-ai/aperture)
- **Pricing:** FREE (self-hosted, MIT license)
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Perplexity (Google AI Overviews planned)
- **Features:** BYOK, self-hosted, multi-language, competitor benchmarking
- **GitHub:** github.com/anyin-ai/aperture

#### AiCMO (AICMO/ai-cmo)
- **Pricing:** FREE (self-hosted) or managed cloud with free tier
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude
- **Features:** Real-time brand monitoring, competitive intelligence, multi-model support
- **Website:** getaicmo.com
- **GitHub:** github.com/AICMO/ai-cmo

#### LLM Visibility Tracker (tanweer4u/llm-visibility-tracker)
- **Pricing:** Runs for under $10/mo via GitHub Actions
- **Coverage:** ChatGPT, Gemini
- **Features:** Automated GEO/AEO brand visibility tracker. Open source

#### AI Visibility Audit (codedpills/ai-visibility-audit)
- **Pricing:** FREE (open source web tool)
- **Coverage:** GEO readiness audit (GEO Score out of 100)
- **Features:** Crawls up to 10 pages, runs 8 static signal analyzers

## SECTION 5: MARKET GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES FOR TEKUP

### Gap 1: No Tool Combines Monitoring + Auto-Fix (UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY)

Current AI visibility tools are almost entirely monitoring-only. TekUp's concept of "auto-fixes issues via schema injection and citation optimization" is first-to-market.

### Gap 2: No SMB-Friendly Pricing for AI Visibility

Cheapest dedicated AI visibility tool is Otterly.AI at $129/mo. Most are $200-$500+. Too expensive for micro-businesses.

**Opportunity:** Sub-699 DKK ($99) pricing for micro-SMBs.

### Gap 3: Traditional SEO + AI Visibility Disconnect

Only 45% overlap between Google rankings and AI citations. No existing tool measures both in a unified dashboard.

**TekUp advantage:** Unified Google + AI search dashboard.

### Gap 4: No Danish/Localized Nordic Solution (UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY)

All major tools are US/global with English as default.

**TekUp advantage:** Danish-first platform with Danish directory coverage and local market expertise.

### Gap 5: No Tool Also Fixes Schemas (UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY)

Schema.org markup is critical for AI visibility but no current tool offers auto-schema injection at the SMB level.

### Gap 6: Citation Accuracy Monitoring (UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY)

AI engines occasionally hallucinate about businesses. No tool monitors accuracy of AI citations — just presence/absence.
