| Element | Adoption % |
|---------|-----------|
| WordPress site (Yoast/RankMath SEO) | ~40-50% |

| Mobile responsive | ~70-80% |
| Has correct hreflang tags | ~5-8% |

### Schema.org & AI Visibility Connection

**Why schema matters for AI visibility:**

1. **ChatGPT** extracts structured data from websites when answering local queries
2. **Google AI Overviews** rely heavily on schema markup for featured snippets
3. **Perplexity** cites pages with clear structured data more frequently
4. **Bing Copilot** uses schema from indexed pages
5. **LocalBusiness schema** is critical for appearing in AI-powered local recommendations

### Schema Adoption Gap

| Schema Type | % of DK SMBs With It | Importance for AI Visibility |
|-------------|---------------------|------------------------------|
| LocalBusiness | 4-7% | 🔴 Critical — without this, AI engines can't verify business details |
| Organization | 5-8% | 🔴 Critical — establishes entity identity |
| Service | 2-4% | 🟡 Important — for service-based businesses |
| Review | 3-5% | 🟡 Important — builds trust signals |
| FAQ | 2-4% | 🟢 Nice-to-have — powers AI answers |
| Product | 6-10% | 🟢 Nice-to-have — for e-commerce |

**Key finding:** ~90% of Danish SMBs lack the schema markup that AI engines need to cite them. This is TekUp's core auto-fix opportunity.
